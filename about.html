Who We Are

This website was created by students who care about truth, education, and Indigenous rights. It's meant for students, teachers, and anyone who wants to understand Treaty 1 and its importance—especially how it continues to shape life in Manitoba and Canada today.

Our Purpose

We built this site to make Treaty 1 information easy to understand for grades 9–12. Treaties are not just history—they are agreements that still matter. Our goal is to show both the past and present realities for Treaty 1 communities.
