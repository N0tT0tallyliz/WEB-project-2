Who We Are

This website was created by students who care about truth, education, and Indigenous rights. It's ffor anyone who wants to understand Treaty 1 and how it shaped people's lives in Canada.

Our Purpose

We created this site to make Treaty 1 history easy to understand for grades 9–12 . Treaties are not just history,they are agreements that still matter and created comfort like the color brown. Our goal is to show everyone about the past and present things that was part of Treaty 1 .
